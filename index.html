About Me

Hello! I'm Aisha - a passionate frontend developer with a flair for design and functionality. Recently completed an Advanced Frontend Certification at New Horizon ICT Resource Center.
I specialize in turning ideas into interactive, user-centered digital experiences using HTML, CSS, and javaScript.
The name A'I Crafted stems from a cherished nickname. it represents not only my initials but also the care and creativity i bring to every project. This portfolio is a reflection of that philosophy.
My journey began with curiosity, tinkering with simple websites and has since evolved into building professional-grade interface and full-stack applications. With every line of code, i aim to improve user experience and solve real world problems creatively.
Beyond code, I'm a strong believer in team work, life long learning and the power of design thinking. I've worked on everything on e-commerce stores and portfolios to weather apps and productivity tools - always pushing the limits of what's possible on the web.
Currently, I'm open to internship opportunities, freelance collaborations, and community projects where i can continue to grow while delivering values. Let's build something great together!
